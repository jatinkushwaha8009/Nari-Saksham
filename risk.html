Sexual Harassment

The Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition, and Redressal) Act, 2013 is an important piece of legislation in India aimed at preventing and addressing sexual harassment of women in the workplace.

The Indecent Representation of Women (Prohibition) Act, 1986

The Indecent Representation of Women (Prohibition) Act, 1986 is an important piece of legislation in India aimed at preventing the indecent representation of women, particularly in the media.
The Act prohibits the creation and publication of any material that contains an indecent representation of women through advertisements, publications, or in any other form, which is likely to deprave or corrupt persons who are likely to see, read, or hear the material.

Equal Remuneration Act, 1976

This act mandates that men and women should receive equal pay for equal work. It prohibits discrimination on the grounds of gender in matters of employment and wages.

Maternity Benefit Act, 1961:

This act provides maternity benefits to women employees, including maternity leave, nursing breaks, and additional provisions for their health and safety during pregnancy and childbirth

Factories Act, 1948:

The Factories Act includes provisions related to the employment of women, such as restrictions on night work and the provision of creche facilities for female workers with children.

Workplace Safety Regulations:

Laws and regulations regarding workplace safety apply to all workers, including women. They establish standards for safe working conditions and may include provisions specific to pregnancy and breastfeeding.
